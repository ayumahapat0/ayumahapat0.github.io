During my junior year of high school, I did my solo piano recital in front of my friends and family. It was an amalgamation of all favorite pieces that I had learned over my time from learning from Dr. Kate Camara.

I started playing piano when I was 9 years old when my parents, with my knowledge, had signed me up to piano lession to someone just a 5 minute walk away from the house. At first, I was disinterested in playing and put forth no effort in practice before my weekly lessons. The lessons were going no where until I started hearing other pianists on youtube and seeing them play these difficult, but beautiful pieces. I love to take on different challenges because not just to prove to myself I can do it, but I love the satisfaction of overcoming them. It gives me the confidence to pursue any other difficult endeavor I will inevitably face in my life. Every since then I started practicing and practicing and the rest is history. Through piano, I was able to learn about self-expression and put my emotions on my sleeve. Even though they did it without my knowledge, my glad my parents did. But I don't want to give them that satisfaction ;).

I participated in many different piano recitals, such as a group performance in tufts University. In addition, I learned many different classical, contemporary, video game, and movie music that I would learn to master.
