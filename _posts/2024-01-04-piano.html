During my junior year of high school, I did my solo piano recital in front of my friends and family. It was an amalgamation of all favorite pieces that I had learned during my time with my instructor, Dr. Kate Camara.

I started playing piano when I was 9 years old when my parents, without my knowledge, signed me up for lessons with someone living 5 minutes from out house. At first, I was disinterested in playing and put forth no effort in practice before my weekly lessons. The lessons were going no where until I started hearing other pianists on Youtube and seeing them play these difficult, but beautiful pieces. I love taking on new challenges; proving to myself that I am capable of overcoming them fives me great satisfaction. It gives me the confidence to pursue any other difficult endeavor I will inevitably face in my life. Ever since then I started practicing and practicing and the rest is history. Through piano, I was able to learn about self-expression and put my emotions on my sleeve. Although they did it without my knowledge, I'm glad my parents signed me up for lessons. But I don't want to give them that satisfaction, though ;).

I participated in many different piano recitals, such as a group performance in tufts University. In addition, I learned many different classical, contemporary, video game, and movie music that I would learn to master.
